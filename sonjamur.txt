This is a recipe for a lentils soup


* carrots 
* lemon
* onions
* salt
* pepper
* chili
* lentils
* coconut milk

1. Fry the onions with the herbs
2. Boil the water and carrots and let it boil for 10 minutes
3. add the lentils and the fried onions etc. and boil for another 15 minutes
4. add the coconut milk and lemon juice and boil for some minutes
5. use the babymixer to mix it all. 

Enjoy!
